主席 (Chair) :
YU-CHIA, KU
古育嘉

學歷：國立陽明交通大學
百川學士學位學程

模聯經歷:
TCYMUN 2020: Chair
TWMUN 2020: Under Secretary-General of Academic
NAMR Ocean Conference: Delegate (Best Delegation)

各位好，我是古育嘉！自高中起，我便深深為模聯入迷 — 不單因伴隨而來的刺激感、能力增長、歸屬感或聲望，更多的是一種對「美感」的執著：對俐落的修辭鋪排、肢體呈現和語境塑造的審美。

模聯是門由國際政治的理想、現實與多元定義，錯綜架構起的語言藝術；更是場在「動」與「反動」間追求體制內創新的運動。希望本次與會的代表們，能體現這串胡謅背後的現象、並從中產生獨立思考的方向感，以及全球性的視野與關懷。

Hi there! My name is Rubin.
To a self-driven, self-proclaimed yet confident Model UN enthusiast like myself, MUN truly is a pursuit of art. Global politics is the flow of information aggregated to an international scale, and any simulation of this process carries the aesthetics of theater – actors who sculpt information with language to express the will and passions of their people and land.

Our mission here, is to expose all that is encapsulated in the process of MUN – perspective, empathy, knowledge, rhetoric, mindfulness, and the philosophy behind the tug of war between the descriptives and normatives of our reality.
